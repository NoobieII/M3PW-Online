Game Synopsis - December 14, 2020

This is the most updated synopsis of the game.

You are a dragon, protecting your species from becoming extinct by humans. The 
setting is the late 17th century, near the beginning of the industrial 
revolution. Your species has lived isolated from humans. The objective is to 
fight humans off by either using your own prowess as a dragon or by creating 
and operating weapons using technology passed on from human remains.

You have the choice to work in a combat division or an engineering division. As 
the game progresses, technology progresses, attacks increase in sophistication 
and newer technologies become discovered. You have the option to interact with 
other animal species.

To find the game's story, check the concepts folder. The story is subject to 
change as the game's path changes.
